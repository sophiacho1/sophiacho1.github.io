About

📚 I’m a rising junior at Harvard, where I major in Computer Science and Statistics and minor in Economics.

📈 I’m interested in the intersection of technology, economics, and public policy. This summer, I’m working for the New York Fed’s Research & Statistics Group.

💡 I’m also active in the start-up space. I currently source and conduct industry research for Plug and Play. Previously, I’ve interned at Treasure Data and Element AI.

🍀 I enjoy staying engaged with Harvard’s CS community. On campus, I serve as a Head Teaching Fellow for CS50 and Peer Concentration Advisor.

🍭 In my free time, you can find me playing the cello, listening to K-pop, or swimming.
